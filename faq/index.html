Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our process, pricing, and services.

Services & Process

Do you install doors that I have already purchased?

Yes, we offer installation-only services for homeowners who have already sourced their own doors. However, we recommend consulting with us first to ensure you purchase the correct sizes and swings for your openings.

What is the difference between specific door core types?

Hollow Core: Lightweight and affordable, but offer poor soundproofing.
Solid Core: Heavier, feel more substantial, and offer significantly better sound reduction and durability. This is our most recommended option.

Do you paint the trim and doors?

Our primary focus is finish carpentry (installation). We do not include painting in our standard bid, but we can recommend trusted local painters or coordinate with yours to ensure a seamless finish.

How long does a typical door replacement project take?

A standard home (10-15 doors) typically takes 2-3 days for full installation, including jambs and casing. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

Pricing & Quotes

Is there a charge for an estimate?

No, our detailed estimates are completely free. We can often provide a ballpark range over the phone or email if you have rough measurements and photos, followed by an in-home visit for a final quote.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, for projects involving materials we purchase, we require a deposit to cover the cost of materials. Labor is typically billed upon completion or in progress draws for larger custom projects.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Denver Metro area, including Golden, Lakewood, Arvada, Englewood, Littleton, Centennial, and Highlands Ranch. See our Locations page for more details.
