## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** Texture is the new color.
2. **Shiplap:** Horizontal boards, nickel gap. Style: Farmhouse, Coastal, Modern Scandi.
3. **Wainscoting:** A broad term for "partial wall" covering.
    - *Board & Batten:* Vertical strips.
    - *Beadboard:* Vertical grooves.
    - *Raised Panel:* Traditional/Formal.
4. **Pricing:** Shiplap is often full-wall (more material), Wainscoting is half-wall (less material, more trim detail).
5. **Verdict:** Modern = Vertical Shiplap or Board & Batten. Traditional = Raised Panel.
