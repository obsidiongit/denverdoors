## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** The most common question we get during estimates.
2. **What is a Slab?:** Just the wood part. Best for: Retrofitting into good frames.
3. **What is Pre-Hung?:** Door + Frame + Hinges attached. Best for: New construction or damaged frames.
4. **Pros & Cons:** Cost vs. Labor difficulty.
5. **Our Recommendation:** Why we almost always recommend pre-hung for the best result.
