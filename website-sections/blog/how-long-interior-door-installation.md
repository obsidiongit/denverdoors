## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** Minimizing disruption to your home.
2. **Phase 1: Measurement & Ordering:** The longest wait (2-4 weeks for materials).
3. **Phase 2: Delivery & Acclimation:** 48-72 hours in your home.
4. **Phase 3: Installation Day(s):** 
    - 1-5 doors: 1 Day
    - Whole home (10-15 doors): 2-3 Days.
5. **Phase 4: Trim & Paint:** The finishing touches.
