## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** Colorado style is changing. Less "Log Cabin", more "Organic Modern".
2. **The Slat Wall:** Vertical thin strips. Adds height and acoustic dampening.
3. **Vertical Tongue & Groove:** Clean, timeless. Great for bathrooms/powder rooms.
4. **White Oak Accents:** The wood of choice for 2024-2025.
5. **Placement:** Behind the bed, entryways, or TV walls.
