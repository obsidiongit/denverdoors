## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** The focal point of the living room.
2. **The "TV Size" Trap:** Always build the opening bigger than your *current* TV.
3. **Cable Management:** Running HDMI/Power behind the wall (smurf tube) before building.
4. **Component Storage:** Mesh doors vs. IR repeaters for cable boxes.
5. **Fireplace Integration:** Balancing heat rules with TV height (Mantel depth matters).
