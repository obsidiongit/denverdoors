# How to Choose Baseboard Height and Profile for Your Space

## SEO
- **Title Tag:** How to Choose the Right Baseboard Height & Profile
- **Meta Description:** Why your baseboards look too small. Learn the golden ratios for baseboard height and how to pick the right profile for your Denver home.
- **Target Keyword:** choosing baseboard height

## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** The subconscious impact of scale.
2. **The 7% Rule:** Baseboards should be ~7% of your ceiling height.
    - 8ft ceiling -> 5-1/4" to 6" base.
    - 10ft ceiling -> 7" to 9" base.
3. **Common Mistakes:** Putting 3" "builder clam shell" in a modern house.
4. **Profiles:** 
    - *Eased Edge:* Modern.
    - *Ogee:* Traditional.
    - *Base Shoe:* To hide uneven floors?
5. **Conclusion:** Go taller than you think.
