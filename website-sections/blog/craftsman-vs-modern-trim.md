## Content Outline
1. **Introduction:** Trim defines the "era" of your home.
2. **The Craftsman Look:** Thick headers, flat stock 1x4 and 1x6, "Archives" style.
    - *Best for:* Bungalows, Wash Park homes.
3. **The Modern Look:** Minimalist. Square baseboards (Eased Edge). "Kerf" details.
    - *Best for:* Lofts, New Builds.
4. **The Blend (Transitional):** How to mix them (e.g., Simple flat stock without the heavy header).
