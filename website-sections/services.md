### Service List

#### 1. Interior Door Installation
Professional installation of solid-core, hollow-core, pre-hung, slab, and custom interior doors. We update your home's look with perfectly fitted doors.

#### 2. Trim & Baseboard Installation
Installation of baseboards, casings, crown molding, and ceiling details. Whether modern, craftsman, or traditional, we ensure clean lines and perfect miters.

#### 3. Custom Built-Ins
Design and construction of mudroom benches, floating shelves, fireplace surrounds, and entertainment centers. Maximize your storage and style.

#### 4. Cabinet Installation
Precision installation for kitchen, bathroom, and laundry cabinets. We work with pre-built or fully custom cabinetry to ensure a seamless fit.

#### 5. Wall & Ceiling Treatments
Shiplap, wainscoting, tongue & groove, paneled ceilings, and barn wood accent walls. Add texture and character to any room.

#### 6. Custom Woodworking
Specialty projects such as accent beams, fireplace mantels, and library ladders. Unique details that make your home one-of-a-kind.
