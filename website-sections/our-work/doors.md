# Interior & Exterior Door Gallery Specifications

## SEO
- **Title Tag:** Custom Interior & Exterior Door Installation Gallery | Denver, CO
- **Meta Description:** Browse our portfolio of interior and exterior door installations in Denver. From solid core upgrades to custom French doors and modern entryways.
- **Target Keywords:** interior doors denver, custom door installation, exterior door replacement

## Content Strategy
Visual-heavy page showcasing the variety of door styles we install. Focus on the upgrade in quality (solid core weight, soundproofing) and style (modern, craftsman, traditional).

### Intro Text
**Headline:** Open the Door to Better Design
**Body:** Doors are the most touched surface in your home. Upgrading from hollow-core builder grade doors to solid-core or custom styles changes the entire feel of a room. Browse our recent installations across Denver, from Arvada to Highlands Ranch.

### Gallery Categories
1. **Solid Core Interior Doors:** Standard matching sets, privacy upgrades.
2. **Feature Doors:** Barn doors, French doors, pocket doors.
3. **Exterior Entryways:** Front doors, patio sliders.

## Layout Reference
- **Hero:** Split screen. Left: "Transformation Gallery". Right: High-quality detail shot of a black hardware handle on a white 5-panel door.
- **Grid Layout:** Masonry grid for general photos to handle different aspect ratios (tall doors vs wide French doors).

## Components to Use
### Gallery Grid
- **Component:** `MasonryPhotoGrid`
- **Props:** `columns={3}`, `gap="large"`
- **Behavior:** Lightbox on click with caption support.

### Before/After Slider
- **Component:** `BeforeAfterSlider`
- **Context:** Show a hollow core 6-panel "colonist" door vs. a new solid core 3-panel craftsman door.

### CTA Section
- **Component:** `BookConsultationCTA`
- **Text:** "Ready to upgrade your home's feel? Quote your door package today."

## Design Instructions
- Use white space generously between grid items.
- All photos should have high-quality alt tags describing the door style (e.g., "3-panel shaker door with matte black hinges").
- Caption overlay on hover: slight dark gradient with text bottom-left.
