1. **Board & Batten:** Entryways, bedrooms, dining accent walls.
2. **Shiplap:** Nickel gap (modern) vs. rustic. Fireplaces, ceilings.
3. **Traditional Wainscoting:** Raised panel, beadboard, chair rail.
4. **Modern Geometric:** Custom slat walls, geometric molding patterns.
