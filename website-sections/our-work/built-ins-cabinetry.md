# Custom Built-Ins & Cabinetry Gallery Specifications

## SEO
- **Title Tag:** Custom Built-Ins, Mudrooms & Shelving Gallery | Denver, CO
- **Meta Description:** See our custom woodworking projects: mudroom lockers, entertainment centers, office built-ins, and fireplace surrounds in Denver homes.
- **Target Keywords:** custom built ins denver, mudroom lockers, custom shelving

## Content Strategy
Demonstrate the functional and aesthetic value of custom millwork. Highlight "solving problems" (storage, awkward spaces) with beautiful solutions.

### Intro Text
**Headline:** Built For Your Life
**Body:** Whether it's taming the chaos of a mudroom or framing a fireplace, custom built-ins add value and order to your home. We design and build specifically for your space.

### Gallery Categories
1. **Mudrooms:** Lockers, benches, shoe storage.
2. **Living Rooms:** Built-in entertainment centers, fireplace mantels and surrounds.
3. **Home Offices:** Bookshelves, built-in desks.
4. **Nooks:** Window seats, under-stair storage.

## Layout Reference
- **Hero:** Full-width image of a stunning library or living room wall unit.
- **Process Highlight:** A section showing a "Sketch to Reality" comparison.

## Components to Use
### Project Spotlight
- **Component:** `ProjectSpotlightCard`
- **Description:** Large card with carousel of 3-4 images for a single complex project (e.g., "The Centennial Library Project").

### Gallery Filter
- **Component:** `FilterableImageGrid`
- **Tags:** Mudroom, Living Room, Office, Bedroom

### Full-Width Callout
- **Component:** `ImageTextSplit`
- **Content:** Detail shot of joinery/hardware. Text about "Paint-Grade vs. Stain-Grade" options.

## Design Instructions
- Highlight the *utility* in captions (e.g., "Hidden charging station," "Soft-close drawers").
- Use warm background colors (`--background-secondary`) for the "Process" section to differentiate it.
