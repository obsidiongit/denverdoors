### Overview
**Title:** Lakewood Door & Trim Installation
**Subtitle:** Modernizing Established Neighborhoods
**Intro:** Lakewood is full of solid, mid-century homes ready for a refresh. We specialize in taking 1950s and 60s ranches and updating them with modern doors and crisp white trim, instantly brightening the space and adding value.

### Lakewood Projects

- **Ranch Updates:** Replacing dark, hollow-core mahogany doors with fresh, painted styles.
- **Wainscoting:** Adding board and batten to hallways and dining rooms in Green Mountain and Belmar.
