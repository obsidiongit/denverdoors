### Our Process
1.  **Measure & Design:** We laser-measure your space and sketch the layout.
2.  **Shop Fabrication:** We build the carcasses and face frames in our shop to minimize mess in your home.
3.  **Installation:** We scribe the unit to your walls and floor for a gap-free fit.
4.  **Trim & Finish:** Crown molding and baseboard tie the built-in into the rest of the room.
