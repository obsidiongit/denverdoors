# Custom Woodworking Specifications

### Overview
**Title:** Custom Woodworking
**Subtitle:** Unique Projects, Handcrafted for You
**Intro:** Have an idea that doesn't fit into a standard category? We love a challenge. From library ladders to fireplace mantels, we tackle unique woodworking projects that require creative problem-solving and fine joinery.

### Specialty Projects
- **Fireplace Mantels:** Rough-sawn beams or refined traditional surrounds.
- **Library Ladders:** Rolling ladders for tall shelving or lofts.
- **Closet Systems:** Cedar lining, custom shelving, and shoe racks.
- **Wine Cellars:** Custom racking and storage for your collection.
- **Hidden Doors:** Bookshelf doors or secret compartments.

### Our Approach
We combine traditional woodworking techniques with modern tools to create pieces that are both beautiful and durable. If you can dream it, we can build it.
