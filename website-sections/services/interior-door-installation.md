# Interior Door Installation Specifications

## Content

### Overview
**Title:** Interior Door Installation
**Subtitle:** Upgrade Your Home with Solid, Sound-Dampening Doors
**Intro:** Interior doors are often overlooked, but they are one of the most tactile elements in your home. We replace hollow, flimsy builder-grade doors with solid-core options that dampen sound, close with a satisfying "thud," and elevate the feel of every room.

### key Features
- **Solid Core Upgrades:** Drastically reduce noise transfer between rooms (perfect for home offices and nurseries).
- **Precision Hanging:** We ensure every door is plumb, level, and swings freely without "ghosting" (swinging open or closed on its own).
- **Hardware Installation:** We install handles, hinges, and latches with perfect alignment.
