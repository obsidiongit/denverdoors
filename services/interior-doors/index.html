Why Replace Your Interior Doors?

Many homes in Denver were built with hollow-core doors. While functional, they do little to block sound and can feel flimsy using them every day. Upgrading to solid-core doors is one of the most impactful changes you can make to your home's interior.

Our Process

  • Precision Measurement: We measure every opening to the 1/16th of an inch to ensure a perfect fit.
  • Custom Sizing: We can cut, plane, and re-size doors to fit settled frames and older homes.
  • Hardware Installation: We install handles, hinges, and latches with perfect alignment.
  • Painting & Finishing: Optional professional painting services to match your trim.

Types of Doors We Install

Solid Core

Heavy, sound-dampening, and durable. The gold standard for bedrooms and bathrooms.

Paneled / Shaker

Timeless designs that add architectural interest to your hallway.

French Doors

Glass-paneled doors perfect for offices and separating living spaces without losing light.
