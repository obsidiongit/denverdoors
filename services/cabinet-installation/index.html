Even the best cabinets need a pro install

Even the most expensive cabinets look cheap if they aren't installed correctly. We provide laser-level precision installation for IKEA, semi-custom, and fully custom cabinetry.

What We Install

Kitchen Cabinetry

Uppers, lowers, islands, and pantries installed with precision.

Bathroom Vanities

Laundry Rooms

Utility cabinets and folding stations to maximize organization.

Office Cabinetry

Why Hire Denver Interior & Doors?

Walls and floors are never straight. We shim and scribe so your cabinets are perfectly level. We ensure heavy cabinets are anchored securely to studs or blocking, and use precision jigs for hardware installation.
