Elevate Your Space with Custom Trim

Trim is the "eyeliner" of a room—it defines the space and covers the gaps. Replacing builder-grade, small baseboards with taller, modern profiles can completely transform the look of your home without minor renovation.

What We Install

Baseboards

From simple 5-inch rectangular stock to ornate traditional profiles.

Crown Moulding

Draw the eye up and add elegance to living rooms and dining areas.

Window & Door Casing

Frame your views properly. We specialize in Craftsman, Colonial, and Modern styles.

Chair Rails & Picture Rail

Classic touches that protect walls and add historic charm.

The Pursuit of Perfection

Bad trim work is obvious—gaps filled with too much caulk, uneven joints, and nail holes that aren't properly filled. We take pride in our "invisible" installation methods, ensuring that once painted, the wood looks like a single continuous piece.
