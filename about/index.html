Our Story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

We’re not just contractors; we’re local woodworkers passionate about elevating the homes of our Denver neighbors.

Since 2017

Built on a Foundation of Respect & Quality

Denver Interior & Doors Co. was founded with a simple observation: homeowners were tired of contractors who didn't show up, didn't clean up, and treated finish work like rough framing.

We set out to change that by focusing exclusively on finish carpentry—the details you see and touch every day. From the solid "click" of a perfectly hung door to the seamless joint of a crown moulding corner, we believe these details define the quality of your home.

Today, we are a dedicated team of master carpenters and installation specialists serving the entire Denver Front Range. We treat every home as if it were our own, using dust containment systems and floor protection to ensure we leave your space cleaner than we found it.

Our Principles

The "Denver Standard"

We hold ourselves to a higher standard of workmanship and service.

Precision First

We don't use caulk to hide bad cuts. We cope our corners and scribe our trim for a fit that lasts.

Communication

You'll never be left wondering when we're showing up. We provide clear schedules and daily updates.

Respect for Home

We treat your home like a sanctuary, not a job site. Dust protection and floor coverings are standard.
