# Product Brief

## What is it?
Denver Interior & Doors Co. is a Denver-based finish carpentry company specializing in interior door installation, trim & baseboards, custom built-ins, cabinet installation, wall & ceiling treatments, and custom woodworking — for both residential and commercial properties across the Denver metro area.

This is a full website rebuild designed to establish a stronger SEO footprint, clearly communicate services, and convert visitors into consultation requests.

## Who is it for?
Denver-area homeowners and commercial property owners who are planning a renovation or upgrade and searching for a trusted, professional finish carpenter. They know what they want but need confidence that the company is credible, skilled, and easy to work with.

## What's the goal?
To convert visitors into leads by getting them to **book a free consultation**. Every page should build trust, showcase craftsmanship, and make it frictionless to take the next step.

## Additional Requirements
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Fast loading — optimized images, minimal JS
- SEO-ready: unique meta titles/descriptions per page, semantic HTML, LocalBusiness + Service schema markup
- Internal linking strategy: Blog → Service pages → Contact
- Style: clean, professional, craft-forward — warm neutrals, strong typography, real photography
- Booking integration via DripJobs (`denverinteriordoors.dripjobs.com`)
